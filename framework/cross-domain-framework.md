## 🧭 Meta‑layer: what this logic actually is

Across all domains, the reasoning follows the same architecture:

1. **Identify the driver**  
   What force is acting on the system?

2. **Identify the thresholds**  
   Where does the system bend, break, or shift modes?

3. **Identify the institutional response**  
   What do governments, markets, or agencies do?

4. **Identify the secondary effects**  
   What cascades outward?

5. **Anchor everything in public data**  
   No vibes. No narratives. No speculation.

This is why the examples look so broad—  
they’re all instances of the same reasoning engine.
